October 9, 2024

Caraga State University - Cabadbaran Campus with CSUCC Energy and Power Research and Innovation Center (CEPRIC) held an initial partnership meeting with Chu Shou Yang, an Associate Professor from the Department of Electrical Engineering at Tatung University, Taiwan. The virtual meeting, conducted via Microsoft Teams, aimed to revive the collaboration between the two institutions, which had been stagnant for several years due to the pandemic. The discussion focused on the continuation of the partnership and explored several key initiatives, including the Taiwan Experience Education Program (TEEP). This funded program allows both students and faculty from CSUCC to engage in immersive learning experiences at universities across Taiwan, including Tatung University. Another highlight of the meeting was the opportunity for research collaboration, particularly in the fields of electrical engineering and industrial technology. CSUCC was represented by several key personnel, including Dr. Deomila B. Mamaoag, Campus Director; Prof. Maria Jovita Serrano, Office Head of Campus Internationalization and Global Engagement; Marisol Jane Beray, TTLO/ITSO technical expert; and Engr. Erickson A. Tacda, the CEPRIC Center Chief. The meeting was highly productive, with both sides expressing enthusiasm for reinvigorating the partnership. Prof. Yang and Tatung University reiterated their commitment to advancing collaborative research efforts and were eager to resume the initiatives that had been paused. CSUCC is equally excited about the potential opportunities for students and faculty through TEEP and the prospective research collaborations.
