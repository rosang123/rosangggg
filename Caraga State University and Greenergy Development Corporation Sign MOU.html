May 20, 2024

In a noteworthy move towards advancing initiatives in renewable energy, Caraga State University (CSU) and Greenergy Development Corporation formally signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) on May 20, 2024, at the Greenergy office located in the Oro Chamber Building, Macapagal Drive, Carmen, Cagayan de Oro City. This collaboration promotes professional development, facilitates innovative research endeavors, and participates in extension projects. Engr. Blondie T. Sanchez, Dean of the College of Engineering and Information Technology (CEIT), Engr. Ianlester L. Romero, Chairperson of the Department of Electrical Engineering, and Engr. Erickson A. Tacda, Director of CEPRIC, represented the forefront of this partnership on behalf of the CSU-Cabadbaran Campus. Greenergy Development Corporation was represented by Engr. Cerael C. Donggay, COO/General Manager, Mr. Frank Jorge G. Cailing, Technical Head, Mrs. Rhona D. Villanueva, HR Manager, and Mr. Del Mark C. Bomediano, HR Coordinator. At the ceremony, Engr. Donggay emphasized the crucial role of research and innovation in renewable energy to combat global warming. He also proposed future collaborative projects in renewable energy with various institutions, including CSU, stressing the importance of united endeavors in confronting environmental issues. He also conveyed his firm belief in the potential of this partnership, highlighting its ability to provide significant opportunities for faculty members to gain industry exposure through immersion and training programs, as well as engagement in practical projects.
