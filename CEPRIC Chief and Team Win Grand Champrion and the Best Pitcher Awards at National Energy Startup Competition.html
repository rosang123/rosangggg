𝗖𝗘𝗣𝗥𝗜𝗖 𝗖𝗵𝗶𝗲𝗳 𝗮𝗻𝗱 𝗧𝗲𝗮𝗺 𝗪𝗶𝗻 𝗚𝗿𝗮𝗻𝗱 𝗖𝗵𝗮𝗺𝗽𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗮𝗻𝗱 𝗕𝗲𝘀𝘁 𝗣𝗶𝘁𝗰𝗵𝗲𝗿 𝗔𝘄𝗮𝗿𝗱𝘀 𝗮𝘁 𝗡𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻𝗮𝗹 𝗘𝗻𝗲𝗿𝗴𝘆 𝗦𝘁𝗮𝗿𝘁𝘂𝗽 𝗖𝗼𝗺𝗽𝗲𝘁𝗶𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻

November 14–16, 2024

This startup competition, which centers on energy and climate entrepreneurship, is a platform to inspire innovators and accelerate ventures that address pressing energy challenges. The team’s outstanding vision and collaborative efforts secured the top prize, and Engr. Tacda grabs his Best Pitcher Award, showcasing the importance of partnerships in driving sustainable energy solutions.

Engr. Tacda led a dynamic team of experts and professionals:

● 𝗘𝗻𝗴𝗿. 𝗠𝗲𝗹𝗴𝗶𝗲 𝗥𝗼𝘀𝗲 𝗖. 𝗔𝗰𝗲𝗿𝗼, an affiliated researcher of CEPRIC;

● 𝗠𝗿. 𝗟𝘂𝗶𝘀 𝗥𝗮𝗺𝗶𝗹𝗼, Founder and CEO of Ramilo Renewable Commodities Corporation and a member of the Negros Oriental Chamber of Commerce and Industry’s Climate Change Committee;

● 𝗠𝘀. 𝗥𝗵𝗲𝗮 𝗣𝗲ñ𝗮𝗳𝗹𝗼𝗿, Senior Resource Mobilization Specialist from CARE Philippines;

● 𝗗𝗿. 𝗠𝗮𝗿𝗶𝗹𝘆𝗻 𝗣. 𝗖𝗮𝗹𝘂𝗯, a faculty researcher from the University of Southern Mindanao with a PhD in Agricultural Engineering, majoring in land and water resources engineering;

● 𝗠𝘀. 𝗠𝗮𝗿𝗶𝗲 𝗔𝗻𝘁𝗼𝗶𝗻𝗲𝘁𝘁𝗲 𝗕𝗮𝗻𝗴𝗮𝗯𝗮𝗻𝗴, Program Officer from Oxfam Pilipinas.

The team presented their innovative startup, 𝙂𝙧𝙚𝙚𝙣𝙚𝙬𝙖𝙗𝙡𝙚𝙨, which focuses on adopting renewable energy through biomass resources. Their project demonstrated the potential of transforming biomass into renewable commodities, contributing significantly to increasing the renewable energy mix in the Philippines.
