July 22-26, 2024

CSUCC Energy and Power Research and Innovation Center (CEPRIC) participated in Brigada Eskwela 2024 conducted by the DepEd Schools of Magallanes District from July 22-26, 2024. This initiative aimed to prepare schools for the upcoming academic year by addressing potential electrical hazards and ensuring a safe learning environment for students. Our team provided essential services, including electrical system assessments, electrical wiring installation and repairs, and other electrical-related services. This effort is part of the CSUCC College of Engineering and Information Technology pre-extension program BUSWAK which stands for 𝘉𝘂𝘪𝘭𝘥𝘪𝘯𝘨 𝘜𝘱 𝘚𝘺𝘴𝘁𝘦𝘮𝘴, 𝘞𝘪𝘳𝘪𝘯𝘨, 𝘈𝘂𝘁𝘰𝘮𝘢𝘵𝘪𝘰𝘯 𝘢𝘯𝘥 𝘒𝘯𝘰𝘸𝘭𝘦𝘥𝘨𝘦 𝘧𝘰𝘳 𝘋𝘦𝘱𝘌𝘥 𝘔𝘢𝘨𝘢𝘭𝘭𝘢𝘯𝘦𝘴 𝘢𝘯𝘥 𝘵𝘩𝘦 𝘓𝘎𝘜 𝘰𝘧 𝘔𝘢𝘨𝘢𝘭𝘭𝘢𝘯𝘦𝘴. The primary goal of this project is to safeguard students from electrical hazards before they return to school, making classrooms a secure and conducive environment for learning. We successfully rendered our services to eight ( 8 ) schools: Magallanes Central Elementary School, Buhang Elementary School, Caloc-an Elementary School, Sto. Niño Elementary School, Dahican Elementary School, Magallanes North Elementary School, Magallanes National High School, and Father Urios Academy of Magallanes Inc. (FUAMI). Our center remains committed to contributing to the improvement of educational facilities and ensuring the safety and well-being of both students and staff.
