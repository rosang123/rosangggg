July 8, 2024

Caraga State University Cabadbaran City (CSUCC) hosted a productive partnership meeting with the Institute of Integrated Electrical Engineers of the Philippines, Inc. (IIEE) and the Association of Building and Industrial Electricians Inc. (ABIE) at the Office of the Campus Director Conference Room last August 8, 2024. The meeting featured insightful discussions led by Professional Regulatory Board Member Jaime V. Mendoza, who provided updates on the REE Board Exam's Table of Specifications. The conversation also explored opportunities for international internships for Electrical Engineering students and industry immersions for our faculty through collaboration with IIEE's network of industries. Additionally, the new requirements for becoming a Professional Electrical Engineer were addressed, along with a review of the impactful extension activities conducted under Oplan Dagitab Brigada Eskwela by CEIT, IIEE, and ABIE. This gathering brought together key figures, including IIEE National Officers, the Board of Governors, and the IIEE-Butuan Agusan del Norte Chapter officers, led by President Engr. Arnel B. Alipao. ABIE Inc. President Rolando Montajes was represented by members of his organization, while CSUCC Campus Director Dr. Deomila Mamaoag was represented by Dr. Erlin S. Rodas, Division Chief for Academic Affairs. Other attendees included CEIT Dean Engr. Blondie T. Sanchez, EE Department Chairperson Engr. Ianlester Romero, CEPRIC Center Chief Engr. Erickson A. Tacda, and faculty members from CEIT.
